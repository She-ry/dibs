Deposit Instructions

To deposit money into your account using MPESA, follow these steps:

  1. Open the MPESA menu on your phone.
  2. Select 'Lipa na MPESA'.
  3. Select 'Pay Bill'.
  4. Enter the Business Number: 12333.
  5. Enter your Account Number: Your phone number.
  6. Enter the Amount you want to deposit.
  7. Enter your MPESA PIN and confirm the transaction.
  8. You will receive a confirmation SMS from MPESA.
